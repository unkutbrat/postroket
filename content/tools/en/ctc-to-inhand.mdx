## How CTC converts to in-hand salary

- **CTC** includes employer costs like PF, gratuity, and insurance.
- Deductions considered here: PF employee portion, professional tax, and optional TDS.
- Monthly in-hand = (CTC / 12) - PF - PT - TDS.

### Tips
- Update PF % when salary crosses statutory cap.
- Add state professional tax when applicable.
